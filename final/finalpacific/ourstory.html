Our Story

Meet the Manager

At Pacific Trails Resort, we believe in the power of nature to heal and inspire. After a transformative hiking experience, where I discovered new depths of myself and found relief from burnout, I felt called to share this rejuvenating connection with others. As the manager of Pacific Trails Resort, I’m passionate about creating an environment where guests can disconnect from the chaos of daily life and reconnect with themselves. Our retreat offers unique experiences that blend adventure, relaxation, and personal growth, all set in the stunning backdrop of nature’s beauty.
